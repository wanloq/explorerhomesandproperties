About Explorer Homes

Building trust, one property at a time.

Our Story

Explorer Homes and Properties was founded over a decade ago with a singular mission: to simplify real estate in Lagos while delivering uncompromising quality. We’ve grown from a small consultancy into a full-service agency, handling residential and commercial sales, leasing, and property management.

Our team combines deep local knowledge with a client‑first approach. Every property is title‑verified, and every transaction is handled with transparency and integrity.

What Drives Us

Trust & Integrity

We conduct every deal with honesty, and our titles are always independently verified.

Quality First

We list only premium properties in prime locations that meet our high standards.

Client‑Centric

From flexible payments to after‑sales support, your satisfaction is our priority.
